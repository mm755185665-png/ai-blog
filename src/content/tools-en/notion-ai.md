Notion AI is a built-in AI feature officially launched by Notion in 2023, embedded directly into its note-taking and project management platform. Unlike independent AI writing tools, the core advantage of Notion AI is "use in situ" - users can complete writing, summarizing, translation and other operations without leaving the current document, which greatly reduces the friction of tool switching.
