Wenxin Yiyan was released by Baidu in March 2023. It is one of the earliest large language model products launched in China. The bottom layer is based on Baidu's self-developed Wenxin large model (ERNIE). The in-depth search function will be launched in February 2025 and will be fully free and open from April. Users can use all functions including AI painting, ultra-long document processing, and online retrieval without paying.
