Dream AI (formerly known as Dreamina) is an AI visual creation platform launched by ByteDance in 2024. Relying on the multi-modal generation capabilities of the Volcano Engine, it provides functions such as Wensheng video, Tusheng video, and AI drawing. As a directly accessible video generation tool in China, it fills the gap where ordinary users cannot use overseas products such as Sora and Runway.
