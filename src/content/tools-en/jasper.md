Founded in 2021, Jasper (formerly known as Jarvis) is one of the most well-known marketing AI writing platforms on the market, focusing on helping brands and marketing teams produce content at scale. Its core advantage lies in brand tone customization - users can upload a brand style guide, allowing the AI ​​to always output content in a unified brand tone. This is a differentiated capability that is difficult to replace by general-purpose AI such as ChatGPT.
