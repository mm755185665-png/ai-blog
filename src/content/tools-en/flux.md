Developed by Black Forest Labs, founded by the former Stability AI core team, Flux quickly became the benchmark model in the field of open source image generation after its release in 2024. Its Schnell and Dev versions are open source under the Apache 2.0 / non-commercial license and can be run natively for free on platforms such as ComfyUI; the Pro version provides the highest quality commercial services through APIs.
