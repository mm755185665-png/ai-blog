Developed by Butterfly Effect Pte. Ltd, Monica is one of the most comprehensive browser AI assistants available. It is embedded in the browser sidebar in the form of a Chrome/Edge extension. Users can complete summary, translation, writing and other operations without leaving the current page, which greatly reduces the cost of tool switching.
