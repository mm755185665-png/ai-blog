Perplexity AI will be launched in 2022 and is positioned as an "AI search engine with cited sources." The core difference is that the source of the information will be marked in each answer, which solves the pain point of traditional LLM being prone to "serious nonsense". It's particularly suitable for situations where you need to quickly verify facts, access the latest information, or do preliminary research.
