DALL·E 3 is the third-generation image generation model released by OpenAI in 2023. Compared with the previous generation, it has significantly improved prompt word understanding and image quality. It is directly embedded in ChatGPT Plus. Users can generate, describe, and modify images in conversations without switching tools, which greatly lowers the threshold for use.
