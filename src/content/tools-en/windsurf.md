Windsurf is built by the Codeium team of AI code completion tools and will be released at the end of 2024. It is positioned as an "AI native IDE" rather than a plug-in. The core difference lies in Cascade Agent - it does not just complete the code, but can understand the entire project context, autonomously plan and execute multi-step modification tasks, similar to an AI collaborator working in the editor.
