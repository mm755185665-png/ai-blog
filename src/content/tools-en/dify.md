Dify, developed by LangGenius, is currently one of the most popular open source AI application development platforms, with more than 60,000 GitHub stars. It integrates workflow orchestration, RAG knowledge base, agent construction and model management into one platform, eliminating the need for teams to build AI infrastructure from scratch.
