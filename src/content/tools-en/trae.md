Trae is an AI programming IDE launched by ByteDance in early 2025. It is deeply customized based on VS Code and focuses on the "AI native" development experience. Compared with competing products such as Cursor and Windsurf, Trae's biggest differentiated advantage is: **Domestic direct connection is available + top models are completely free**. There is no need to access the Internet scientifically or pay a monthly fee to call Claude 3.7 Sonnet and GPT-4o.
